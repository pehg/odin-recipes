White russian (The big Lebowski)

Description

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz. vodka
  • 1.5 oz. coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz. half and half milk
  • 1 small pinch salt (optional)
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a rocks glass with ice,
  2. Stir briefly to incorporate
  3. Do not shake. Do not garnish. Drink and enjoy.
